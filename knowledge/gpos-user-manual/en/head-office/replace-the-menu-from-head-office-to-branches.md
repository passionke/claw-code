# Replace the menu from Head Office to Branches

**Official docs:** https://gpos.co.th/en/user-manual/head-office/replace-the-menu-from-head-office-to-branches

## Steps / Content

Replace the menu from the head office to branch locations allows you to update the entire product list at once while completely removing the previous menu. This ensures that all branches use the same standardized menu, reduces duplication, and enables efficient centralized management.

1. Click Item > Menu Template Management

2. Click +Add Menu template

3. Set Template Name

4. Select the store branch to export the selected items to that branch.

5. Add Items to select the desired products, then click Save.

6. Click Save and Exit.

7. Click Apply to Store > Replace Menu then click Next.

8.Verify the information, then click Distribute.

9.Wait until the process is completed successfully.
