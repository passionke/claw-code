# Adding Items from Head Office to Branches

**Official docs:** https://gpos.co.th/en/user-manual/head-office/add-items-from-headquarters-to-branches

## Steps / Content

Adding items from the Head Office to branches helps distribute products quickly and maintain standardization across all locations. It reduces duplicate data entry for each branch and allows centralized control of items and pricing efficiently.

1. Click Item > Menu Template Management

2. Click +Add Menu template

3. Set Template Name

4. Select the store branch to export the selected items to that branch.

5. Add Items to select the desired products, then click Save.

6. Click Save and Exit.

7. Click Apply to Store > Add New Items then click Next.

8.Verify the information, then click Distribute.

9.Wait until the process is completed successfully.
