# How to Sort Payment Channels

**Official docs:** https://gpos.co.th/en/user-manual/store-management-back-office/how-to-sort-payment-channels-back-office

## Steps / Content

The GPOS payment channel configuration after connecting with KBANK allows users to easily and quickly set up the payment system without requiring technical knowledge. This enhances transaction efficiency and reduces configuration errors. Additionally, it provides flexibility and adaptability for future use of the payment system.

### 1. Log in to the Back Office

Go to login.gpos.co.th , enter your email and password, and click Log In.
Once logged in, select the menu “Store Management” .

### 2. Go to Payment Method Settings

Select the menu “Charge Management”. Then, click on “Payment Management” to access the payment channel settings page.

### 3. Sort Payment Channels After Connecting to KBANK

After successfully connecting the system to KBANK, a new payment method named “Prompt Pay” will appear in the system. Select the “Order” menu to sort the payment channels in the desired order.

### 4. Sort the Payment Channels

Once you click Order, the system will show up/down arrows that allow you to sort the payment channels. Click the up or down arrows to arrange the payment methods in your preferred order, making it easier to use. After completing the sorting, click the “Save” button to confirm the settings and order of the payment channels.
