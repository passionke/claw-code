# Staff Login via POS Device

**Official docs:** https://gpos.co.th/en/user-manual/staff-management/lock-staff-login-via-pos-device

## Steps / Content

1. Enter the employee email and password, then tap “Remember Password” before logging in.

2. Tap to select the store.

3. Enter the preset PIN code.
