# Adding tags to products

**Official docs:** https://gpos.co.th/en/user-manual/product-management/adding-tags-to-products

## Steps / Content

Helps customers with food allergies easily identify the ingredients in the products or dishes they order.

1. Click “Items” > “Item Attribute”

2.Click on “Tag Management” at the top.

3.Click “Add Group”, then press the “+” icon. After that, enter the option group name and click “OK”.

4.Click “Add Tag”, enter the tag name such as Pork, Chicken, Beef, Shrimp, etc., then enter the display order and click “OK”.

5.Click “Linked Items” to assign the desired products, then click

6.You can click ... to edit, Sort, or delete the menu.
